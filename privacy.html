Privacy Policy

Nerd Owl Integrated Services (“iMart.Africa”, “we”, “us”, “our”) provides a platform for its users to request and purchase items from service providers and have them delivered to their doorstep (“Services”). At iMart, one of our core values is transparency, and we owe you (“User”) a duty to tell you how we collect, use, and share your personal information.

Personal information means information that identifies you, including your name, email address, telephone number, and any other personal data collected.

We recommend you read this Privacy Policy (“Policy”) carefully to understand your rights and our duties towards managing your personal data in addition to our Terms & Conditions. If you have any questions about either of these documents, please contact us at support@imart.africa

1. Notification of Changes to Privacy Policy

  1. We review this Privacy Policy annually to ensure compliance with NDPR and applicable standards.
  2. We may update this Policy to reflect changes in our data practices, legal requirements, or service offerings. If updated, the revised Policy will be published on our website/apps.

  1. We collect only essential personal information, including but not limited to when you:
  2. 2.1. Buy a product or service from us.
  3. 2.2. Register for any of our services.
  4. 2.3. Subscribe to newsletters or promotions.
  5. 2.4. Contact us for information, questions, or complaints.
  6. 2.5. Take part in promotions or surveys.
  7. 2.6. Use or browse our website/app.
  8. 2.7. With your consent, we may collect information from other organizations, including fraud prevention agencies and credit reference agencies.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

  1. We may use cookies and web beacons to improve your experience, understand your use of our platform, and personalize content and services. Cookies do not damage your device and can be managed through your browser settings.

4. Use of Your Personal Information

  1. We may use and analyze your information to:
  2. 4.1. Provide and improve delivery and marketplace services.
  3. 4.2. Keep you informed about new products and services.
  4. 4.3. Personalize your user experience and our services.
  5. 4.4. Comply with applicable laws and regulations.
  6. 4.5. Authenticate users and verify identities.

  11. 4.10. Contact you via phone, email, or SMS regarding services or promotions.
  12. 4.11. Provide vendors with aggregated, non-identifiable reports.
  13. 4.12. For any other purpose with your consent.

5. Consent

  1. By creating an account, using our platform, or engaging with our services, you consent to the collection and processing of your personal information as described. If you wish to withdraw your consent or opt-out, please contact us at [insert support email].

6. Lawful Basis for Processing

  1. We process your data to fulfill our contractual obligations to you, including processing orders and deliveries.

7. How We Collect Your Information

We collect information:

  • Directly from you (registration, forms, surveys, customer service requests).
  • Automatically via your use of our platform (location, device details).
  • From third parties with your consent or as allowed by law.

8. Types of Information Collected

  1. We may collect:
  2. 8.1. Personal details: name, date of birth, gender, address, phone, email.
  3. 8.2. Order and delivery details, including location data.
  4. 8.3. Device and technical data (OS, device identifiers, network info).
  5. 8.4. Payment and transaction data.

  7. 8.6. Social media interaction data if linked with your account.
  8. 8.7. Survey or optional demographic data if provided.

9. Third-Party Technologies

We may integrate or link to third-party services (e.g., payment processors, logistics partners, Facebook, Instagram) with separate privacy policies, which you should review.

10. Location Services

To facilitate deliveries, we may request access to your device’s location via GPS, IP address, or Wi-Fi. You may adjust these permissions on your device settings.

11. Communication Monitoring

We may record communications made via the iMart platform (calls, messages) for safety, dispute resolution, quality assurance, and service improvement, with your consent.

12. Surveys

We may occasionally request optional surveys to enhance our services. By participating, you consent to the collection and processing of the provided data.

13. Disclosure of Personal Information
