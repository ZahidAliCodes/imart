Our Story

I’m an amazing cook but market errand is not my forte, because of the long queues, endless price negotiations, crowded stalls, and transportation.

One weekend, I was having a good time with my friends and a relatable question popped, who runs your errands for you? The conclusion of that conversation made me realised it was a major problem that needs innovative solution.

Young professionals, parents, and even students wants to escape the stress of traditional errands, without compromising good prices, freshness, neatness and swift delivery.

I took it personal and decided to work with a few dedicated shoppers to test if we could build a system that works. After several meetings, learning from every delivery, hiccups, and feedback from customers and vendors, we discovered it wasn’t just about building an app or connecting vendors but rewiring how convenience could be made accessible while supporting local businesses.

With a better understanding of the needs, we focused on building an efficient, easy-to-use system that ensures shoppers handpick quality items for customers while our delivery partners bring them to doorsteps without hassle.

We wanted something simple yet effective; saving people time while ensuring fair prices without the stress that comes with running market and personal errands.

That’s how iMart was born. A simple, effective platform that brings together supermarkets, local markets, and pharmacies to deliver fresh groceries, medicines, and household essentials straight to your doorstep.

We built iMart so you can spend more time with the people you love while we handle your market runs empathy and commitment.

Olufemi Stephen Adeosi

Mission

At iMart, our mission is to make shopping stress-free, reliable, and convenient. We are committed to saving you time while supporting local vendors and empowering our community through a system built on care, efficiency, and trust.

Vision

Our vision is to become the most trusted and accessible marketplace for everyday essentials, transforming how people shop across Africa. We aim to build a world where getting your daily needs is seamless, affordable, and convenient.
